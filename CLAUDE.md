# Health Design Sprint - Course Development

## Project Context
2-week M4 elective at UVA teaching design thinking + AI prototyping.
7 students across UVA Charlottesville and INOVA campuses.
Course dates: Feb 16-27, 2026.
Instructor: Matt Trowbridge

## Key Principles
- Knowledge, Skills, Mindset framework for every session
- Process over polish - prototypes test insights, not solutions
- Virtual-first delivery (recordings become materials)
- Fun and not too intense - achievable in 2-3 hour work sessions
- 14-phase workflow students experience through demo + real project

## File Structure
- SOURCE/schedule.yaml: Master schedule (source of truth)
- SOURCE/exercises/: Exercise specifications
- GENERATED/student-materials/: Handouts, guides for students
- GENERATED/instructor-materials/: Facilitator guides, scripts
- templates/: Blank forms and templates
- transcripts/: Zoom recordings and voice agent logs
- projects/: Team workspaces

## Generation Commands
When asked to generate materials:
1. Read SOURCE/schedule.yaml for structure and content
2. Output to appropriate GENERATED/ subfolder
3. Use MDP brand voice: clear, encouraging, practical
