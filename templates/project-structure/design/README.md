# Design

Define and ideate phase (Days 3-5)

## Subfolders

- `problem-statements/` - Drafts and iterations of your problem statement
- `hmw-statements/` - "How Might We" explorations
- `concepts/` - Early ideas, sketches, brainstorms

## Key Deliverable

`design-brief.md` - Your final design brief (due Day 5)

## Tips

- Generate many ideas before converging
- Document why you chose certain directions
- The design brief is a hypothesis, not a contract
