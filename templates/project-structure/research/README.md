# Research

Discovery phase materials (Days 1-3)

## Subfolders

- `interviews/` - Raw interview transcripts and notes
- `synthesis/` - Patterns, insights, journey maps, affinity diagrams
- `sources/` - Articles, papers, references you're drawing from

## Tips

- Save interview transcripts immediately after conducting them
- Keep raw data separate from your analysis
- Name files clearly: `interview-sarah-2026-02-17.md`
