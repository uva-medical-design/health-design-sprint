What You'll Learn

Over two weeks, you'll move through the full design sprint cycle—twice. By the end, you'll have:

  • Conducted stakeholder interviews using AI-enhanced analysis
  • Built functional prototypes with no-code and AI platforms
  • Tested your concepts with real users
  • Presented to an expert panel

The goal isn't polished products—it's validated insights and the confidence to keep building.
